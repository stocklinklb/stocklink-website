Pricing FAQ

Questions about cost

Why isn't there a fixed price listed?
Every store has a different number of products, categories and pages, which changes the amount of work involved. We'd rather give you an honest, accurate quote than a generic number that doesn't reflect your actual project.
Is there anything to pay upfront just to get a quote?
No. Reaching out and getting a quote is free. You'll know the full cost before any work begins.
Can I upgrade my plan later as my store grows?
Yes. It's common to start with a Starter site and expand it as your catalog and needs grow — we can plan for that from the beginning if you'd like.
