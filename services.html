Services

Everything your store's website needs, handled in one place

From the first sketch to a live, searchable catalog — here's exactly what's included.

01 · Website Design

Design that looks like a real software company built it

We design every page around your products and your brand — minimal, clean layouts with clear typography and no clutter. Nothing generic, nothing that looks like a template.

  • Custom layout built around your product range
  • Consistent branding across every page
  • Clear visual hierarchy so customers find products fast
02 · Website Development

Built by hand, not assembled from plugins

We write clean, lightweight code for every site — no bloated page builders slowing things down. That means fewer bugs, faster pages, and a site that's easier to maintain long-term.

  • Hand-built HTML, CSS and JavaScript
  • No unnecessary plugins or dependencies
  • Organized, documented code you can hand to anyone
03 · Product Catalog Websites

Your inventory, organized and browsable

Phones, tablets, smart watches and accessories — all displayed with photos, names, and prices, so customers can browse before they ever call or walk in.

  • Category-based product grids
  • Simple structure for adding or removing items
  • Direct contact buttons on every product
04 · Responsive Design

Perfect on the phone customers actually browse from

Most of your customers will visit from a phone. Every StockLink site is designed mobile-first and tested across screen sizes, with no broken layouts or awkward zooming.

  • Mobile-first layouts
  • Tested on phones, tablets and desktops
  • No horizontal scrolling or layout shift
05 · Performance Optimization

Fast enough that customers never think about it

Slow websites lose customers before they even see your products. We optimize images, code and loading behavior so your site feels instant.

  • Optimized, lazy-loaded images
  • Minimal, efficient JavaScript
  • Proper caching for return visits
06 · SEO Optimization

Set up to be found by people already searching

We handle the technical SEO basics — page titles, descriptions, structured data and clean URLs — so search engines understand your store from day one.

  • Unique titles and meta descriptions per page
  • Structured data for search engines
  • Clean, readable URLs and heading structure
07 · Hosting Guidance

Clear guidance so your site stays online and reliable

Choosing hosting can be confusing. We recommend reliable options for your needs and walk you through getting set up — or handle the setup for you.

  • Hosting recommendations that fit your budget
  • Domain and SSL setup guidance
  • Support getting everything connected
08 · Maintenance & Updates

We stay reachable after launch, not just before

Need a new product added, a price changed, or something adjusted? Reach out any time — we keep your site current so it keeps working for you.

  • Ongoing content and price updates
  • Direct line to StockLink after launch
  • Guidance as your store and catalog grow
