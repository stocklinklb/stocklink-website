About StockLink

A new company, built around one clear idea

Phone stores in Lebanon deserve websites as fast and professional as any global tech company's — without the agency runaround or the outdated templates.

Who we are

We're a newly launched studio, and we're upfront about that

StockLink was started to solve one specific problem: phone stores, mobile retailers and electronics shops across Lebanon often have no website at all — or one that's slow, outdated, and hard to update. We're a new business, so instead of leaning on claims we can't back up, we focus on doing the work well and being transparent about where we're starting from.

"A store's website should feel as trustworthy as walking into a well-run showroom — clear, fast, and easy to navigate."
What drives us

Our mission and vision

Our mission

To give phone stores in Lebanon a genuinely fast, professional online catalog — so they can spend less time repeating prices on the phone and more time serving customers in person.

Our vision

A future where every local phone store — not just the biggest chains — has a website that reflects the quality of what they actually sell.

Why we started

We kept noticing the same gap: great phone stores with no way for customers to browse online. StockLink exists to close that gap, one store at a time.

Our philosophy

No fake reviews, no invented statistics, no shortcuts. We'd rather earn trust through the quality of the work than manufacture it with claims we can't stand behind.

What we commit to

Commitments we hold ourselves to

Quality first

Every site is designed and built with care, not assembled from a generic template. If it doesn't meet our own bar, it doesn't ship.

Speed by default

We treat performance as a feature, not an afterthought. A slow site loses customers before it ever gets the chance to sell.

Helping local businesses grow

We build for Lebanese phone stores specifically — their products, their customers, and the way they actually sell.

Let's build something honest and fast together

We're new, focused, and ready to give your store the attention it deserves.
